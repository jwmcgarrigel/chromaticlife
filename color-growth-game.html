ℹ️ How to Play
Game Modes
🌱 Easy
Smaller 30×22 board. Growth rate 0.025, mutation chance 6%. No speed slider or pause — compete for best time.
🎮 Default
Standard 40×30 board. Growth rate 0.04, mutation chance 12%. Compete for best time and lowest clicks.
💀 Insane
Larger 50×36 board. Growth rate 0.06, mutation chance 20%. Shop items cost 30% more and randomly go out of stock for 5–15s after purchase. Random chaos spores drop every 40–60s automatically.
🤝 Duo Challenge
Shared 52×38 board for two players. Growth rate 0.075, mutation chance 20%, spores every 20–35s. Each player has their own inventory and points. The shop stays live — no pausing. Actions sync in real-time. Combined score and clicks hit the Duo leaderboard under both names.
🌈 Free Play
Standard board with speed slider and pause. When you clear the board a mushroom animation blooms and a new board spawns. Track cumulative stats with the Stats button.
How to Play
👆
Click a color
Removes all connected cells of a similar color (flood fill). Earn 1 pt per cell + small group bonus.
🌱
Growth
Colors spread into empty space each tick. Some growths mutate into a nearby hue. Clearing the board wins.
Shop Tools
💣 Bomb, 🔀 Scrambler, and 🧹 Wiper are available in all modes. 🧱 Wall, 🍄 Spore, and 💥 Wall Bomb are Free Play only.
💣
Bomb
Blasts a radius-5 circle. Also destroys walls inside the blast. Leaves an invisible 5-second no-growth / no-mutation zone in the cleared area.
🔀
Scrambler
All cells in a 5×5 area centred on click, plus all cells connected to them, shift to a color that already exists elsewhere on the board. Great for breaking up dominant colors.
🧹
Wiper (nerfed)
Sweeps 3 tiles wide horizontally AND 3 tiles wide vertically centred on click, removing the target color and any color touching a removed cell. Also destroys nearby walls.
🧱
Wall
Places a 3-tile horizontal or vertical barrier that blocks color from spreading through it. Choose direction with the buttons that appear. Destroyed by bombs and wipers.
🍄
Spore
Drops a chaos zone (5×5, 10s) where almost all growth produces wildly different colors. Visible as animated rings with countdown.
💥
Wall Bomb
Destroys ALL walls within a large radius (8 tiles). Does not remove color cells. Pure wall demolition.
